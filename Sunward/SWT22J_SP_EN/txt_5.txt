1.3 NOTE 1
The interchange shown in column ⑻ indicates if the part could interchange with other part. The column is written in
N、Y、T、P、I、H、*.These letters’s mean are following:
N：Indicates the two parts can’t be interchanged;
Y：Indicates the two parts are interchanged utterly;
T：Indicates the part in whole replaces the former part used in former machine;
P：Indicates the part replaces the former part being not used any more;
I：Indicates the part replaces the former part but doesn’t replace opposite.
